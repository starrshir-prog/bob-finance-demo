Headcount Plan vs. Actual

Real-time variance between your workforce plan and HR reality — with peer benchmarking powered by the Bob dataset.

The pain

Finance builds hiring plans in spreadsheets. HR lives in Bob. When a hire slips, a band changes, or a backfill opens — the financial model is instantly stale. FP&A spends 2–4 days every month manually reconciling HR reality with their plan.

The solution

A live Headcount Plan vs. Actual layer inside Bob Finance — sliceable by department, cost center, and role — with a benchmark overlay powered by HiBob's 4,400+ company dataset. FP&A and HR share one plan, one source of truth. No exports, no email chains.

What I'd measure
  • Time-to-reconcileHours Finance spends aligning HC data with HR — target: ~3 days → same-day
  • Forecast accuracyPlan vs. actual headcount cost variance at quarter close — target: <3%
  • Benchmark engagement% of Finance users acting on a peer insight within 30 days
